# Role Search Config: Hospital Medicine

## Target Societies
Search for **named roles** at these organizations (officer, board member, council member, committee chair/member — not general membership alone):

- Society of Hospital Medicine (SHM)
- Society of General Internal Medicine (SGIM)
- American College of Physicians (ACP)
- Alliance for Academic Internal Medicine (AAIM)

## Target Journals
Search for **editorial board or editor roles** at these journals:

- Journal of Hospital Medicine
- Journal of General Internal Medicine
- Annals of Internal Medicine
- JAMA Internal Medicine
- American Journal of Medicine
- BMJ Quality & Safety

## Leadership Roles
Include if held:

- Division Chief / Division Director
- Program Director (Residency or Fellowship)
- Department Chair / Vice Chair
- Section Chief
- Center Director (e.g., Center for Quality Improvement, Patient Safety Center)
- Chief Medical Officer (hospital or health system)
- Quality Improvement Officer / Patient Safety Officer
- Utilization Review Committee Chair
- NIH Study Section member or chair
- FDA Advisory Committee member
