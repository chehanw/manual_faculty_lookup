# Role Search Config: Geriatric Medicine

## Target Societies
Search for **named roles** at these organizations (officer, board member, council member, committee chair/member — not general membership alone):

- American Geriatrics Society (AGS)
- Gerontological Society of America (GSA)
- American Federation for Aging Research (AFAR)
- Association of Directors of Geriatric Academic Programs (ADGAP)
- International Association of Gerontology and Geriatrics (IAGG)

## Target Journals
Search for **editorial board or editor roles** at these journals:

- Journal of the American Geriatrics Society (JAGS)
- Gerontologist
- Age and Ageing
- Journals of Gerontology
- Journal of Nutrition Health and Aging
- Aging Clinical and Experimental Research

## Leadership Roles
Include if held:

- Division Chief / Division Director
- Program Director (Residency or Fellowship)
- Department Chair / Vice Chair
- Section Chief
- Center Director (e.g., Center on Aging, Memory Care Center, PACE Program Director)
- Geriatric Consultation Service Director
- Palliative Care Program Director
- NIH Study Section member or chair
- FDA Advisory Committee member
